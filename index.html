D.E.A.D's Revenue Across The World

This is D.E.A.D's intake of revenue by Nation and Area. Each Nation or Area call size represents the proportion of revenue coming from that area

- LateOrders: The proportion of orders delivered to a Nation with a delivery time of over 20 days

.

- HighValueOrders: The number of orders delivered to a Nation with a Cart Price of over 25,000 Copper Pieces
